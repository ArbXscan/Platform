# Introduction

ArbXscan is designed as a long-term Web3 analytics platform.

Rather than focusing solely on arbitrage scanning, the platform aims to evolve into a comprehensive ecosystem for blockchain intelligence, market research, and professional analytics.

This document outlines the strategic roadmap and long-term objectives of the project.

---

Deliverables:

- Project documentation

- Core infrastructure

# Long-Term Objectives

ArbXscan aims to become:

- The most trusted Web3 analytics platform.
- A leading arbitrage intelligence platform.
- A reliable source of blockchain market data.
- A preferred analytics solution for traders, researchers, developers, and institutions.

---

# Strategic Goals

- Support all major blockchain ecosystems.
- Integrate with leading decentralized exchanges.
- Expand cross-chain market coverage.
- Continuously improve data quality.
- Deliver high-performance user experiences.
- Build a sustainable and scalable platform.

---

# Success Indicators

The project will be considered successful when:

- Users trust the platform as a primary source of market intelligence.
- The platform supports a broad range of blockchain ecosystems.
- Data remains accurate, transparent, and reliable.
- New features can be added without major architectural changes.
- ArbXscan becomes a recognized brand within the Web3 ecosystem.

---

# Guiding Vision

Every version of ArbXscan should move closer to one goal:
