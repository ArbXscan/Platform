# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## Overview

**Product:** ArbXscan

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Draft

**Last Updated:** June 2026

---

# Product Summary

ArbXscan is a professional Web3 Analytics & Arbitrage Intelligence Platform designed to aggregate, validate, and visualize blockchain market data across multiple ecosystems.

The platform enables users to discover arbitrage opportunities, compare decentralized exchange prices, monitor liquidity, and analyze cross-chain market activity through a unified dashboard.

ArbXscan is an information platform only.

It does not execute trades, hold user funds, or provide financial advice.

---

# Problem Statement

Web3 data is fragmented across numerous decentralized exchanges, blockchain explorers, aggregators, and bridge protocols.

Users often need to switch between multiple platforms to gather information before making trading decisions.

This process is inefficient, time-consuming, and increases the risk of acting on incomplete or inconsistent data.

---

# Solution

ArbXscan consolidates verified market information from multiple trusted sources into one professional analytics platform.

By validating and presenting data in a consistent format, ArbXscan enables users to analyze market conditions more efficiently and identify potential arbitrage opportunities.

---

# Product Objectives

The primary objectives of ArbXscan are:

- Aggregate blockchain market data from multiple sources.
- Deliver accurate and transparent analytics.
- Simplify cross-chain market analysis.
- Help users make informed decisions.
- Provide a scalable foundation for future Web3 intelligence features.

# Business Model

Potential revenue streams may include:

- Premium subscriptions
- Professional analytics plans
- Enterprise solutions
- API access
- Institutional services

No business model is implemented in the current planning phase.

---

# Product Principles

Every feature added to ArbXscan must satisfy at least one of the following goals:

- Improve data accuracy.
- Increase transparency.
- Enhance user understanding.
- Improve platform performance.
- Simplify complex blockchain information.

---

# Out of Scope

The following are intentionally excluded from ArbXscan:

- Custodial wallet services
- Automated trading bots
- Asset management
- Financial advisory services
- Exchange operations
- Token issuance

---

# Success Definition

ArbXscan succeeds when users can access accurate, transparent, and actionable Web3 analytics from a single platform without relying on multiple disconnected tools.
